about us

Multitex Sourcing Ltd (MSL) was established in 2006 with mission and vision to support in apparel Business in Bangladesh.This company is right now one of leading Apparel exporters and manufacturer in Bangladesh.

Almost all of the customers want to begin the transition to this new ecological, sustainable world, so we are fully focusing and concentrating more value to your brands for safer world.

The idea is to use recycled fabric, linings and accessories and fabrics are made from bottles, organic/eco fillings or organic cotton fabrics.

How we work

Multitex Sourcing Ltd is recognized as one of the reputed apparel and well-established Export oriented manufacturing, sourcing agent and Inspection Company. We are situated in the famous commercial area at Uttara Dhaka in Bangladesh. The company specializes in areas of product development, production & timely deliveries, online & post production inspections, co-ordination & communication etc. We feel proud that we deliver the best total package of managing, manufacturing, quality control and shipping garments throughout the industry.
